In this session, you will:

  1. Knit the same document to a different output format from an extension package (bookdown::html_document2)

  2. Knit a new document to a slide presentation (powerpoint_presentation)

  3. Export your figures to a place you can find on your computer so you can share and re-use them. (can I use knitr::fig.path with here package??)

  4. Add figure captions using knitr chunk options.

  5. Add cross-references in text to figures.

  6. Make a multi-panel plot.
